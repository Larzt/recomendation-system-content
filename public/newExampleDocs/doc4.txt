Generative AI is reshaping healthcare by discovering new drugs and designing personalized treatments. Using advanced algorithms and machine learning models, this technology analyzes vast medical datasets to predict molecular interactions and generate potential compounds. What once took years of laboratory research can now be achieved in weeks. Generative AI also assists in diagnostic imaging, identifying subtle patterns that human eyes might miss. Beyond research, AI tools can support doctors with predictive analytics and patient monitoring. Despite ethical and privacy challenges, generative AI stands as one of the most promising breakthroughs in medical innovation.
